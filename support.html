Everscroll for macOS

Everscroll lives in your menu bar and captures long, scrolling screenshots — then lets you beautify, split into social carousels, extract text, and redact private info, all on your Mac.

Getting started

  • Click the Everscroll icon in the menu bar (or press ⌥⌘A) to start a scrolling capture. Drag the region, then scroll the page — Everscroll stitches it into one tall, sharp image.
  • The menu also has Screenshot, Extract Text, Image to Text, Image to Carousel, and Pick Screen Color.
  • First run asks for macOS Screen Recording permission — required for any capture tool. Grant it, then quit & reopen.

Features

Edit & beautify

Short captures open pre-beautified (background, padding, shadow). Add arrows, shapes, text, numbered steps, and mosaic blur from the toolbar; tweak the look in the right inspector, or save a look as a preset.

Social carousels

Split one long image into evenly-sized slides, or pick several images (Image to Carousel → select multiple) and each becomes one slide — rendered to Instagram / LinkedIn / TikTok / Pinterest / X specs.

Extract & translate text

OCR any capture or image (40+ languages, on-device). In the editor, the Copy Text tool lets you drag a box over text to grab just that part. Tap Translate to translate via macOS.

Auto-redact

Everscroll flags likely emails, phone numbers, and card numbers and offers one-tap Redact all — a pixel-destroying mosaic, all on-device. Toggle it in Settings → After capture.

FAQ

Why does it need Screen Recording permission?

macOS requires it of every app that captures the screen. Everscroll uses it only for the captures you start — see the Privacy Policy.

Where are my files saved?

By default to ~/Downloads/Everscroll/ (auto-save can be turned off, or the folder changed, in Settings → After capture). Save As and carousel export ask you where.

Is it really all local?

Yes — your screenshots never leave your Mac. The only network use is an optional macOS Translation (Apple's service) and a small carousel-spec config download. Details in the Privacy Policy.

Free trial & purchase

Everscroll Pro includes a 14-day free trial; it's a one-time purchase, not a subscription. Already bought it? Open the unlock screen and choose Restore Purchase.

Which macOS does it need?

macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later. Translation needs macOS 14.4+.
